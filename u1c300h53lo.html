4. Built for Open Data and Open Research

Unlike arXiv preprints, Authorea documents are web-native and allow you to integrate data, code and all the material needed to reproduce scientific results. This enables transparency and reproducibility, the pillars of the scientific method. Do you see that data icon to the left of the Fig. 4, below? Click on it to gain access to the data "behind the figure" - there is a CSV and an Jupyter Notebook associated with the figure.
