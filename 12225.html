Web-native and web-first.

There is growing consensus in scholarly communication circles that academic publishing needs to move "beyond the PDF" (see the manifesto of Force 11). We neither have to invent a new format, nor reinvent an existing one t. It is clear that the format of the paper of the future is HTML \cite{Goodman}. As such, the arXiv of the future will have to enable creation and/or ingestion of papers in HTML format.
